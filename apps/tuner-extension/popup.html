Project Prosodia Affective Space

This utility enables you to "reverse the flow" of narration tuning. By playing professional voice clips, podcasts, or audiobook clips, you can capture speech characteristics, assign Valence-Arousal-Tension vectors, layer acoustic offsets, and save them directly to a local project file.

Affective Wire-Format Syntax:

[V: <valence> A: <arousal> T: <tension> ...tags] <Text>

Tuning Tags Reference:

  • SB: Speed Bias (adds/subtracts from baseline speech speed)
  • GB: Gain Bias (adds/subtracts from baseline speech volume)
  • PB: Pause Multiplier (scales typographical punctuation pauses)
  • LK: Speaker Lock (overrides dynamic voice blending)
  • PN: Pronunciation Override (spells words phonetically)
